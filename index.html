WHY TAKE A BREAK?

Whether studying, working on a project, or doing any other type of mentally taxing activity, it is essential to take regular breaks. Studies have shown that even a quick 5 minute break can make a positive impact.

Unfortunately, in today's fast paced world, people often neglect taking breaks. This can have negative impacts including burnout, hampered performance, mental fatigue, and stress. Stress is also linked with anxiety, insomnia, and depression.

On the bright side, taking regular breaks has the opposite effect. Benefits include improved:

  • mood
  • productivity
  • energy
  • stress prevention
  • success
  • general well-being

The most effective way to take a break varies based on the individual, but in general, methods involving relaxation, socializing, and movement are best. The goal of this website is to have a collection of quick break techniques in one place for easy and accessible use. All you have to do is take 5 minutes.
